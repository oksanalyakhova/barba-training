Leonardo Raphael Michelangelo Donatello

Donatello, nicknamed Don or Donnie, is a fictional superhero and one of the four main characters of the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles comics and all related media. He is co-creator Peter Laird's favorite Turtle. In the original comics, all four of the Turtles wear red masks. In the animated cartoons, he dons a purple mask.
